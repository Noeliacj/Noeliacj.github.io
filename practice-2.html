PD Controller:

I have implemented a PD, without an integral part, and I have adjusted the values ​​so that it would solve the error and follow the line without over-oscillating too much.

Tests carried out:

The tests that I have been carrying out during the practice are:

  • Circuit Simple
  • Circuit Montmelo
  • Circuit Montreal
  • Circuit Nürburgring

In all these circuits (without the Ackerman version) the code works, in some of them sometimes gets lost and takes longer to reach the goal, but in all of them it ends up achieving the objective.

Practice difficulties:

Some of the problems experienced in carrying out the practice are:

  • Values for PD Controller.
  • Image perception of the line (HSV).

It has been quite difficult in terms of time to find values ​​for the PD that were good in all the circuits and that did not overoscillate and be continuous.

As for perception, I started trying to analyze the image with RGB but I couldn't get it to work well. In the end, I have implemented perception with HSV, managing to analyze the image well and obtaining the center of the image (with the X coordinate to later calculate the error).
