Welcome to my website!

Hello! My name is Noelia Casado, and I warmly welcome you to my website dedicated to the exciting field of Mobile Robotics. Throughout this space, I will share my projects, experiments, and advancements in this discipline, along with useful resources and practical demonstrations of the exercises I have carried out during my studies.

Mobile robotics is a constantly evolving field where innovation and creativity come together to solve real-world problems, from designing autonomous vehicles to service robots that interact with us in everyday life. Here, you will find:

  • Detailed descriptions of my practices.
  • Images and videos of the robots in action.
  • Reflections and insights on the development of autonomous robots.

This site is designed for both students and enthusiasts who are eager to learn more about the fascinating world of mobile robots. Explore, learn, and join me on this exciting journey into the future of technology!
